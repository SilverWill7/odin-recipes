Traditional Chilaquiles

This is a traditional chilaquiles recipe that is great to eat for breakfast

with fried eggs or for dinner as a main course with a salad.

Ingredients:

  • 8 tomatillos
  • 1 onion
  • 1 jalapeno pepper
  • Cilantro
  • 4 gloves of garlic
  • Salt
  • half cup of vegtable oil
  • 12 corn tortillas
  • 1 cup of Jack Cheese
  • 1 cup of shredded Monterey Jack Cheese
